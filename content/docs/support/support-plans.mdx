## PlanetScale Cloud Support PlanetScale

PlantScale offers three options for support depending on your plan.

### Community

* Access to the [PlanetScale GitHub Discussion](https://github.com/planetscale/beta/discussions) board
* Ability to submit support tickets


### Standard

* Access to the [PlanetScale GitHub Discussion](https://github.com/planetscale/beta/discussions) board
* Email and ticketing support
* 2 business day turn around time for all requests

### Premium

* Access to the [PlanetScale GitHub Discussion](https://github.com/planetscale/beta/discussions) board
* Email and ticketing support
* Premium SLAs

| Priority | SLA            |
|----------|----------------|
| Urgent   | 1 hour         |
| High     | 4 hours        |
| Normal   | 1 business day |
